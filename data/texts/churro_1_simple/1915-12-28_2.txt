Losses in the British Merchant Fleet.
London, December 26th.
The Reuter's Bureau reported that the losses in the British merchant fleet reached 16 million pounds over the last 10 months. The estimated value of the losses was 4% of its worth.
Reports from enemy command posts.
Vienna, December 28th.
From the war correspondent's office, we report the following:
France
December 27th, at 3 AM.
During the night, there has been no significant activity in Lothringen, where our artillery continues to bombard the enemy positions in the area of Bioncourt and Grémeceh. A bomb hit a building near Château Salins.
December 27th, at 11 AM.
In Belgium, the enemy's efforts have failed to disrupt our artillery. In the area of the Somme and Reims, enemy batteries fired on our troops. This resulted in the destruction of a blockhouse built by the enemy between the first enemy line and the 195th corps. Our artillery fired on the enemy positions in the Champagne area, resulting in the destruction of a large number of enemy tanks. We captured a large number of enemy soldiers, including infantrymen, gunners, and machine gunners. The enemy is now retreating from the front line due to heavy bombardment.
Belgian Report. Heftier Artillery Battle
On the entire Belgian front, our artillery has destroyed or damaged many enemy tanks and guns. Our infantry also captured many enemy soldiers. The enemy is retreating in disarray.
Oriental Army. The situation on our front remains unchanged.
Report from England and neutral post traffic.
London, December 26th.
On the English Channel, near the port of Calais, a large ship, the "Tubantia", was captured. It was loaded with ammunition and supplies for the enemy, which had been prepared by the enemy for a large offensive operation against us. The "Tubantia" was taken to Dover, where it was unloaded.
Report from the French General Headquarters
Paris, December 26th.
The French General Headquarters reports that they have received intelligence that the enemy is planning a large offensive operation against us. They are preparing their defenses accordingly. They have received intelligence that the enemy is preparing to attack our army in the area of the Somme and Reims. They are also aware that the enemy is preparing to attack our army in the area of the Champagne. They are also aware that the enemy is preparing to attack our army in the area of the Loire valley.
